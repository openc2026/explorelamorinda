The Trail Neighborhood and Reliez Valley share a defining feature: the Lafayette-Moraga Regional Trail runs right through them. This paved multi-use path creates something rare in Lamorinda — a linear community where neighbors meet on foot and bike, not just in cars. If outdoor access and an active lifestyle matter to you, this is your neighborhood.

## The Character

The trail changes everything. Morning joggers wave to dog walkers. Kids bike to school along the path. Neighbors who might never meet in a typical suburb become regulars on the trail. It's Lamorinda's most connected neighborhood — literally.

Reliez Valley extends north toward Briones, offering more rural feel and larger lots. The Trail Neighborhood proper is tighter-knit, centered on the path that gives it its name.

## Typical Homes

- **Style:** Mix of 1960s-80s ranches, some newer construction
- **Size:** 1,600–3,500 sq ft typical
- **Lots:** Variable — some modest, some substantial
- **Condition:** Ranges from original to updated

More variety than Burton Valley, less consistency than Happy Valley. You'll find modest homes and impressive ones, sometimes on the same street.

## Price Range

**$1.3M – $2.5M** for most homes

The Trail Neighborhood offers relatively accessible Lafayette pricing. Smaller or older homes start around $1.3M. Updated homes near trail access cluster around $1.8M–$2.2M. Larger Reliez Valley properties with acreage push toward $2.5M+.

## Who Lives Here

- **Active families** — trail access is the draw
- **Outdoor enthusiasts** — hikers, bikers, runners
- **Pet owners** — the trail is perfect for dogs
- **Mix of ages** — from young families to active retirees
- **Value seekers** — more house per dollar than Burton Valley

People here chose lifestyle over prestige. They want to step out their door and onto a trail.

## Walkability & Amenities

**Walkability: Moderate to High** (along the trail corridor)

- Direct access to Lafayette-Moraga Regional Trail
- Trail connects to downtown Lafayette (north) and Moraga (south)
- Walk/bike to Lafayette Reservoir
- Drive to BART, schools, most services

The trail is the amenity. 7+ miles of paved path connecting Lafayette and Moraga, with spurs to the reservoir and beyond.

## Schools

Trail/Reliez Valley addresses typically feed into:
- **Springhill Elementary** or **Lafayette Elementary** — varies
- **Stanley Middle School**
- **Acalanes High School**

School assignments vary — check specific addresses.

## The Vibe

This neighborhood attracts people who actually use outdoor space, not just admire it. You'll see neighbors in running shoes more than heels. Saturday mornings mean trail traffic, not country club brunches.

It's less status-conscious than other Lafayette neighborhoods. People here care about trail conditions, park maintenance, open space preservation. The community organizes around shared outdoor passion.

## Considerations

- **Trail can be busy** — weekends see heavy foot/bike traffic
- **Variable housing stock** — no consistent architectural identity
- **Some areas feel less cohesive** — more transition zone than nucleus
- **School assignments split** — check carefully
