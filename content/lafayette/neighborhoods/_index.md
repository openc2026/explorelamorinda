Lafayette offers the widest variety of neighborhoods in Lamorinda. From the walkable downtown core to secluded hilltop estates, there's a Lafayette that fits almost any lifestyle. The common thread? Great schools, easy BART access, and that elusive California mix of suburban comfort and natural beauty.

## Lafayette's Main Neighborhoods

### [Downtown Lafayette](/lafayette/neighborhoods/downtown/)
The only truly walkable neighborhood in Lamorinda. Restaurants, shops, and BART on foot — plus a real sense of place.

### [Burton Valley](/lafayette/neighborhoods/burton-valley/)
Lafayette's family heartland. Flat streets, excellent elementary school, and the closest thing to a traditional suburban neighborhood in Lamorinda.

### [Happy Valley](/lafayette/neighborhoods/happy-valley/)
Quiet, secluded, and prestigious. Larger lots, hillside settings, and a sense of being apart from it all.

### [Trail Neighborhood / Reliez Valley](/lafayette/neighborhoods/trail-reliez-valley/)
Where the Lafayette-Moraga Trail brings community together. Diverse housing, connected feel, and outdoor access at your doorstep.
