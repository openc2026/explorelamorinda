Sanders Ranch is Moraga's crown jewel — a gated community of custom homes on large lots, backing up to open space and offering the kind of privacy and prestige you'd expect to pay Orinda prices for. It's where Moraga's most successful families go when they want space, security, and community.

## The Character

Sanders Ranch feels set apart. The gate creates an immediate sense of privacy. Inside, the roads wind past custom homes on generous lots, many with views of the surrounding hills. It's Moraga's answer to Happy Valley or Sleepy Hollow — but with even more space and often better value.

The neighborhood has its own private park, swim club, and community events. Residents know each other. There's a cohesion that gated communities sometimes achieve when done right.

## Typical Homes

- **Style:** Custom homes — Mediterranean, Traditional, Contemporary, Ranch
- **Size:** 3,000–6,000+ sq ft
- **Lots:** Large (0.5–2+ acres typical)
- **Condition:** Generally excellent; most homes built 1980s-2000s

No tract housing here. Every home is custom. Some properties include equestrian facilities — there's room for horses in Sanders Ranch.

## Price Range

**$2.5M – $6M+**

Entry-level Sanders Ranch (smaller home, smaller lot) starts around $2.5M. The bulk of inventory clusters in the $3M–$4.5M range. Premium properties — largest lots, best views, newest construction — push toward $6M and beyond.

## Who Lives Here

- **Successful professionals** — tech executives, entrepreneurs, senior partners
- **Families wanting space** — room for kids, maybe horses
- **Privacy seekers** — the gate and acreage appeal
- **Some empty nesters** — large homes, low maintenance grounds

Sanders Ranch is affluent but not flashy. People here have achieved success and want to enjoy it privately.

## Walkability & Amenities

**Walkability: Low**

- Walk within the community (private roads, low traffic)
- Walk to the private Sanders Ranch park and pool
- Drive to everything else — schools, shopping, BART
- Trail access to open space

The neighborhood is self-contained. Kids can bike the private roads safely. Beyond the gates, you drive.

## Community Amenities

Sanders Ranch HOA provides:
- **Private park** with playground and picnic areas
- **Swim club** with pool
- **Community events** throughout the year
- **Gated security** with controlled access

## Schools

- **Los Perales Elementary** (typically)
- **Joaquin Moraga Intermediate School**
- **Campolindo High School**

Same excellent Moraga schools — the district boundary, not neighborhood prestige, determines assignments.

## The Vibe

Sanders Ranch attracts families who want it all: top schools, large lots, privacy, community, and room to spread out. It's less socially competitive than some Orinda neighborhoods — there's space, both physical and psychological, to live on your own terms.

The community is engaged but not overwhelming. HOA events bring people together without being mandatory. Neighbors look out for each other but respect privacy.

## Best Time to Visit

**Late March into April:** Sanders Ranch is at its most photogenic right now. The surrounding hills are emerald green, wildflowers are pushing up along the trails, and the private park comes alive with families enjoying the spring weather. If you're house-hunting, this is when the neighborhood sells itself — the views, the open space, the sense of being tucked into nature while still minutes from everything. Drive in (guest entry at the gate) and you'll immediately understand the premium.

## Considerations

- **HOA fees** — required for community amenities
- **Gated community dynamics** — works for some, not others
- **No BART** — long commute if working in SF
- **Fire risk** — interface with wildland (mitigated but real)
- **Premium pricing** — Moraga's most expensive neighborhood
