### What These Numbers Mean

- **Median price** — half of homes sold above, half below. A more honest signal than average in a market with a wide price range.
- **Active listings** — homes currently on the market (not in contract). Higher numbers = more buyer choice; lower numbers = sellers have leverage.
- **Days on market** — median time from list to accepted offer. Under 14 days indicates a hot market; over 30 days indicates a slower one.
- **Trend direction** — qualitative read on whether the local market is tightening (less inventory, faster sales), steady, or softening.

### How to Read This Right Now

Lamorinda continues to perform as a resilient family market. Inventory remains tight by historical standards across all three towns, especially in desirable school boundaries. Orinda is the tightest of the three on days-on-market, reflecting commuter demand. Lafayette has the deepest inventory among the three but also the highest median price. Moraga remains the best value on a per-square-foot basis, mostly because of the missing BART station — a trade-off that suits many families.
