Orinda isn't one neighborhood — it's a collection of distinct communities, each with its own character. Whether you're looking for walkability to schools, a swim club lifestyle, or estate-level privacy, there's an Orinda neighborhood that fits.

## Orinda's Main Neighborhoods

### [Glorietta](/orinda/neighborhoods/glorietta/)
Family-friendly flatland neighborhood centered around Glorietta Elementary. Walkable streets, engaged community, and the most accessible price point in Orinda.

### [Ivy Drive](/orinda/neighborhoods/ivy-drive/)
Quiet, established neighborhood near downtown with easy BART access. Mix of original 1950s-60s homes and renovated properties.

### [Orinda Country Club](/orinda/neighborhoods/orinda-country-club/)
The original Orinda — gracious homes around the historic country club. Golf, tennis, and a strong social fabric.

### [Sleepy Hollow](/orinda/neighborhoods/sleepy-hollow/)
Orinda's most prestigious neighborhood. Secluded valley setting, excellent elementary school, swim & tennis club, and homes from $2M to $12M+.
