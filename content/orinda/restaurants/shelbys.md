Shelby's anchors Theatre Square's dining scene with upscale California cuisine. It's the spot for a proper dinner before the movies, a business lunch with style, or a date night without driving to Walnut Creek.

## The Experience

White tablecloth territory for Orinda. The menu leans California with seasonal ingredients, and the execution is reliable. It's been a Theatre Square staple for years. The location — right by the historic Orinda Theatre and next to Anytime Fitness — makes it the natural choice for pre-movie dining.

## What People Order

- Lobster ravioli (a longtime standout)
- Seasonal California dishes
- Wine list and craft cocktails

## Good to Know

- **Reservations recommended** — book via their website or call ahead
- Monday is lunch only (11:30am-3pm)
- Full dinner service Tuesday through Sunday
- Good pre-theatre timing — they understand the movie crowd
