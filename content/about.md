Explore Lamorinda is your independent guide to Lafayette, Moraga, and Orinda — the three communities that make up California's Lamorinda region.

## Our Mission

We created this guide because we love Lamorinda and want to share it. Whether you're a longtime resident, a newcomer, or just visiting, our goal is to provide accurate, useful, and up-to-date information about everything the area has to offer.

## What We Cover

- **Neighborhoods** — Detailed guides to every corner of Lamorinda
- **Restaurants & Shops** — Honest recommendations from people who eat and shop here
- **Getting Around** — Practical advice on BART, parking, and transportation
- **Real Estate** — Market insights and neighborhood profiles
- **Events & Activities** — What to do and when to do it
