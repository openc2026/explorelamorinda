## By town

### Lafayette neighborhoods

Lafayette has the most variety — a genuine walkable downtown, a flat family neighborhood, prestigious hillside estates, and a trail-connected community. See the full [Lafayette neighborhoods index](/lafayette/neighborhoods/) for the deeper guide, or jump straight to a neighborhood:

- [Downtown Lafayette](/lafayette/neighborhoods/downtown/) — walkable, BART-adjacent.
- [Burton Valley](/lafayette/neighborhoods/burton-valley/) — flat, family-centric.
- [Happy Valley](/lafayette/neighborhoods/happy-valley/) — private, hillside.
- [Trail / Reliez Valley](/lafayette/neighborhoods/trail-reliez-valley/) — trail-connected.

### Moraga neighborhoods

Moraga's four main neighborhoods range from school-centered Campolindo to the gated Moraga Country Club. Full guide at the [Moraga neighborhoods index](/moraga/neighborhoods/).

- [Campolindo](/moraga/neighborhoods/campolindo/) — athletic, school-centered.
- [Moraga Country Club](/moraga/neighborhoods/moraga-country-club/) — gated golf community.
- [Rheem Valley](/moraga/neighborhoods/rheem-valley/) — mid-town convenience.
- [Sanders Ranch](/moraga/neighborhoods/sanders-ranch/) — newer construction.

### Orinda neighborhoods

Orinda's neighborhoods skew toward established, wooded settings with some of Lamorinda's largest lots. Full guide at the [Orinda neighborhoods index](/orinda/neighborhoods/).

- [Glorietta](/orinda/neighborhoods/glorietta/) — south Orinda swim-club community.
- [Ivy Drive](/orinda/neighborhoods/ivy-drive/) — walkable, near-downtown.
- [Orinda Country Club](/orinda/neighborhoods/orinda-country-club/) — historic estates.
- [Sleepy Hollow](/orinda/neighborhoods/sleepy-hollow/) — north Orinda woods.

## How to choose

A few useful framings if you're trying to narrow down:

- **Walkability matters most?** Start with Downtown Lafayette, Ivy Drive, or Rheem Valley.
- **Top-rated elementary school priority?** Match the [Schools page](/schools/) to the neighborhood's feeder pattern in the table above.
- **Larger lots and privacy?** Look at Happy Valley, Orinda Country Club, Sleepy Hollow, or Sanders Ranch.
- **Gated/country-club lifestyle?** Moraga Country Club or Orinda Country Club.
- **Newer construction?** Sanders Ranch (Moraga) has the most newer homes; otherwise Lamorinda is dominated by 1950s–1980s housing stock.
