It is Friday, July 10, at 5:47 AM, and the dog is at the deck door *exactly* [as predicted last night at 10:11 PM](/blog/thursday-bell-brothers-the-empty-blue-chair/). The mother lets him out. She stands in the doorframe in the fleece she pulled off [the hook by the door](/blog/thursday-bell-brothers-the-empty-blue-chair/) — *the fleece that draped the blue chair over the handbag at 7:41 PM* — because it was still there and because the fleece by the door is *the correct fleece* at 5:47 AM on the third morning of a four-day recalibration. She does not fold it back up. *That fleece is on the hook until Thanksgiving*. She [wrote that rule last night](/blog/thursday-bell-brothers-the-empty-blue-chair/) without writing it. This is *day three*, and day three is the day the recalibration [softens on schedule](/blog/thursday-bell-brothers-the-empty-blue-chair/).

The ridge is *doing the pre-Diablo lift* — the [peach band](/blog/june-gloom-the-other-side-of-the-tunnel/) is a thinner ribbon this morning, four days on the wrong side of the [Bell Brothers Thursday](/blog/thursday-bell-brothers-the-empty-blue-chair/), one week further into the July dry-gold.
