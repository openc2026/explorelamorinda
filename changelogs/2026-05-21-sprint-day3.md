## Decisions / Notes

- **Tone consistency:** Each town's page follows the same nine-section template (Essentials → Overview → History → Neighborhoods → Schools → Things to Do → Dining → Real Estate Snapshot → Who Loves It Here → FAQ → Related). This makes the three town pages directly comparable and reinforces the brand's information architecture.
- **Additive only:** All previously-existing content was preserved verbatim or absorbed into the expanded sections. No paragraphs, lists, or links were removed.
- **FAQ Q&As are rendered twice** on each page: as plain markdown (good for human readers and AEO/quote extraction) and as schema via the `faq:` frontmatter (which the Day-1 schema partial converts to FAQPage JSON-LD). This double-rendering is intentional and follows current AEO best practice.
- **No CTA partial inserted on _index.md pages.** Markdown doesn't support `{{< partial >}}` shortcodes natively (only `{{< shortcode >}}` calls), and these pages don't currently have CTA banners injected from the layout. Day 4 will address newsletter signups and additional CTA placement via the partials, per the sprint plan — appropriate to handle there.
- **Real Estate FAQ ships with 6 Q&As** as specified; the other four pages ship with 6 in `faq:` frontmatter and 5 in the on-page Q&A list (kept tight for readability; full set is in frontmatter for schema).
