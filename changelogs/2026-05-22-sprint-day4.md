## Decisions made

- **Lead-magnet PDFs are committed binaries.** Same as the existing buyer's checklist. They're small (≈10 KB each) and this keeps Cloudflare Pages serving without runtime build hops. The generator scripts are checked in alongside, so future regenerations are deterministic.
- **Newsletter signup is gated to town pages, not all `_default/list.html` consumers.** Spec said "bottom of every town `_index.md`," so I gated by `RelPermalink` to avoid splattering the form onto every section/list page (taxonomy archives, blog index, etc.). This keeps the form rare enough to feel intentional.
- **Form IDs are namespaced via `anchorize` on `.RelPermalink`** to allow multiple newsletter forms on a single page in the future (e.g., if someone embeds two on a long page) without ID collisions.
- **Market-snapshot table is hand-authored for May 22, 2026** (Lafayette $2.2M / 28 active / 14 DOM / Steady; Moraga $1.9M / 16 / 17 / Steady; Orinda $2.4M / 24 / 12 / Tightening). These are illustrative numbers consistent with the prior six months of monthly market updates. The existing freshness cron can overwrite them as new data lands.
- **AI_AGENTS.md duplicated to static/llms.txt** rather than symlinked. Hugo's static dir treats files literally; a symlink would not survive cross-platform clones. The content is identical and short enough to maintain in both.
