## Why This Pairing

Today is the Tuesday after Memorial Day. Yesterday was the holiday's last
day; tomorrow is the first "normal" workday in five. The blog post captures
the cultural-anthropology specifics of *today* — the half-unpacked car, the
twelve-instructional-days classroom vibe, the empty afterschool pool, and
the upcoming graduation-party weekend — in the established voice.

The site improvement makes the companion *practical* page genuinely useful
for the next three weeks: the Getting Around section is exactly where a
new-to-Lamorinda commuter, a relative arriving for graduation, or a parent
wondering when to do their Trader Joe's run goes for actual logistical
advice. Retiring the Memorial Day weekend section yesterday would have been
fine; doing it today and *adding* a graduation-weekend section plus a
last-two-weeks-of-school pickup-chaos section converts stale freshness into
real local utility for the window ahead.

The blog drives interest via voice and timeliness; the Getting Around
improvement makes the traffic productive when readers click through.
