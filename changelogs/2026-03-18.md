# Site Update — 2026-03-18

## Changes Made

### 1. Added Local Lore to Orinda (`/orinda/`)

New "Local Lore: The Name" section explaining that Orinda was named by Alice Marsh Camron after the pen name of 17th-century English poet Katherine Philips, "The Matchless Orinda." The poet died in 1664 but her pen name lives on in a California suburb 350 years later.

### 2. Added Local Lore to Moraga (`/moraga/`)

New "Local Lore: The Name" section covering the Moraga family history — José Joaquín Moraga founded SF's Presidio in 1776, his descendants received the land grant in 1835, and the Moraga Adobe (built 1841) is the oldest building in the East Bay. Ties the naming to Joaquin Moraga Intermediate School.

### 3. Enhanced Social Bird restaurant page

Added new "The Location" section describing the corner spot on Restaurant Row and the prime patio people-watching. Added spring evening tip about booking ahead for outdoor seating.
