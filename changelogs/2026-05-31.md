Olympic Boulevard staging area (Lafayette) is the northern end; EBMUD Valle Vista staging area is the southern end. The Lafayette-Moraga History Trail (lmhistorytrail.org) describes the same trail as a "7½ mile" pedestrian and bicycle path "along St. Mary's Road through the communities of Lafayette and Moraga" — consistent with EBparks at a rounded figure. AllTrails lists an 11.6-mile route which appears to be an out-and-back hike record rather than the trail length itself; not authoritative for trail-length on the page.
