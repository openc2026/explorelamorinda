## Why This Pairing

Pickleball is a real, observable phenomenon in Lamorinda right now with active civic
debate across California (Carmel ban Nov 2025, San Jose noise barriers, court closures
elsewhere). The blog post captures the cultural moment in the site's voice; the
things-to-do page fills a genuine information gap (where can I actually play?) and
gives the blog post a useful destination for the call-to-action. Together: one
revertable PR that improves both content and information value.
