## Site improvement

### `content/things-to-do/briones-regional-park.md` (NEW PAGE)

- Created the dedicated Briones Regional Park page that was previously a bare textual mention in `things-to-do/_index.md` and several `lafayette/` pages without any landing target. This was a real internal-link gap — the page is referenced as a major Lamorinda outdoor attraction across at least 16 existing content files but had no destination.
- Real research, verified against EBRPD (ebparks.org/parks/briones):
  - **Park scale:** 6,255 acres, established by EBRPD in October 1967.
  - **Hours:** 8 AM to sunset, daily.
  - **Fee:** $5 per vehicle when kiosk attended; $25 per bus; cash only (the non-obvious gotcha).
  - **Bear Creek Staging Area** addressed as 6800 Bear Creek Rd, Lafayette — the local-side trailhead.
  - **Alhambra Creek staging area** noted as fee-free alternative entry from the Martinez side.
- Three signature hikes documented with mileage and elevation gain: Old Briones Road to the high bench (~2 mi / 500 ft), Mott Peak loop (~4.5 mi / 1,000 ft, Mott Peak summit 1,424 ft), and the Briones Crest / Briones Peak loop (~6.5 mi / 1,400 ft, Briones Peak 1,483 ft — highest point in the park).
- Bring-with list, cattle/grazing/horse/bike etiquette section, full four-season "when to go" guide, and a timely **June 2026 seasonal note** covering the Campo (June 7) and Miramonte (June 14) post-grad-party Sunday crowd patterns at the Bear Creek lot.
- Driving directions from Lafayette, Orinda, Moraga, and the Caldecott Tunnel.
- Cross-links: lafayette-reservoir, lafayette-moraga-trail, blog/trail-etiquette, blog/sunday-hike-the-recovery-trail (today's post — the page and post co-link), things-to-do, lafayette.
