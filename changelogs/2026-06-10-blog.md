The page is the canonical destination for anybody Googling "Moraga concert series" between now and August 20 — and the highest single-day search volume on this page will be tomorrow (Thursday June 11) by people who decided this morning that they want to go to opening night and are looking up where, when, what to bring. The Opening Night Survival Guide is the answer to that exact search intent. The stale "now through June 10" framing being replaced was a small but real credibility issue going into peak-traffic day.
