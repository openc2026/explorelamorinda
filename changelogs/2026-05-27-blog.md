## Why This Pairing

The blog post and the site improvement are *the same story* told to two
different audiences:
- The **blog post** captures the *host's* Wednesday — the cultural ritual of
  graduation-party prep three days out, in the same observational voice as
  the rest of the recent run. It's the social-anthropology piece for
  someone who lives here and recognizes the pattern.
- The **restaurant improvements** capture the *visitor's* Wednesday — when
  the relatives are about to fly in, the host is busy mowing lines into the
  lawn, and someone needs to actually book a Saturday-night dinner that
  isn't going to be 9:15pm at a Walnut Creek chain.

Both serve the same three-weekend graduation-party window
(May 30, June 6, June 13). The blog post ends with a wink toward the
restaurant pages; the restaurant pages now have the practical content the
wink implies.

This also drains the "freshest" near-term item from yesterday's Next
Priorities queue — closing the loop without piling on more
half-implemented seasonal content.
