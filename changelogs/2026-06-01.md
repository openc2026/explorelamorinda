## Notes

- **The Pennini's / Michael's correction is the most substantive content fix in the Moraga restaurant cluster in several weeks.** The page had been quietly inaccurate on a real diner-impact question: "If I'm planning a Friday date night at Michael's, what should I expect, and what number do I call?" Under the old framing, a reader would conclude Michael's is Pennini's bar side with burgers, accessible from the same phone, on the same schedule. Under the corrected framing, they'd know to call a different number for reservations at a fine-dining room that's only open two nights a week. That's the kind of factual gap that erodes a local-info site's credibility — exactly the gap a daily-freshness pass should be hunting for.
- **The dual-concept framing's longevity in our copy is a useful lesson.** That description likely matched reality at some point in the last decade, but the operator's web presentation has since clarified into two separate offerings. Local-info content can drift from current-truth even when the underlying business is stable — the operator's framing of itself can change without any closure or ownership event, and that's worth catching on verification passes, not just hours and addresses.
- **Restaurant `lastVerified` cadence audit is now better-supported.** Three of the Moraga restaurant pages (Loard's, Town Bakery, Chef Chao) have now verified clean on hours/address/phone over a 10–11 day interval. Pennini's verified clean on hours/address/phone over the same interval — only the *framing* needed correction, which is a different failure mode and not detectable by frontmatter alone. Suggests the audit should track two cadences: a fast cadence for hours/phone/address (where Yelp scrape changes show up) and a slower cadence for editorial framing (where a deeper read-through of the operator's own site is needed). The latter is probably a 60–90 day cadence in the absence of any signal.
- **The seasonal-not-dated lesson is now applied in three places** (Saint Mary's, Loard's, Chef Chao). Worth promoting from a per-page habit to a near-rule for any new body-copy seasonal block: default to month or season framing, escalate to specific-event framing only when the event itself is the subject of the block.
- **Cross-link mesh** did not change materially today. Could have linked Pennini's to Moraga Commons or to Saint Mary's (since both are within a few blocks and the date-night Michael's traffic likely overlaps with Saint Mary's home events). Worth doing on the next Pennini's pass once the corrected framing has settled.
