## Decisions made

- **Sitewide Place schema gated by URL.** Only `/lafayette/`, `/moraga/`, `/orinda/` get the Place block; their child pages do not. Cleaner than tagging frontmatter and matches the "town-level entity" model Google prefers.
- **`og:type=article` only on `/blog/*`.** Everything else is `website`. Simple, defensible.
- **Title strategy on home page** is now `Explore Lamorinda — Discover the best of Lafayette, Moraga & Orinda` (Site.Title + tagline), 60 chars — within the ≤70 budget.
- **Fixed the test-site regexes** instead of forcing the layout to emit quoted attrs. The minified output is correct HTML5; the test was wrong. Means future minifier changes won't reintroduce noise.
- **Did not delete or edit existing page content.** Only added the `last_updated` field per the additive-only constraint.
