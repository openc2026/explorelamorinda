**Structural changes:**
- New title with stronger SEO: "Lafayette Restaurants — A Local's Guide to Restaurant Row & Beyond"

- Added 2026-Restaurant-Walk newcomers: **Western Flyer** and **360 Gourmet Burritos** (verified via web search of the 2026 Restaurant Walk roster) — these were missing from the old list
- Restored visibility to spots that were on the old list but never developed: **Tropa, Sideboard, Lafayette Public House, Barranco, Uncle Yu's, The Park Bistro**
- Linked out to each per-restaurant detail page (Postino, Social Bird, Batch & Brine, Hideout Kitchen) — the old index never linked to its own detail pages

## Why this combination

- **Sunday's blog post closes the May graduation arc.** The arc ran ten consecutive days (5/22–5/31). Yesterday's payoff post needed an epilogue or it would read as a one-day-too-loud ending. Today's "morning after" gives the relatives, the lawn, and the graduate a real handoff to summer, and explicitly hands the next ~6 weeks of blog content to the queued early-summer themes.
- **The Lafayette restaurants index was the most embarrassingly thin top-level page on the site** and was almost certainly hurting SEO for the highest-search-volume Lamorinda query. Three of the three town-restaurant indexes should look like siblings; with today's change they finally do. The Moraga index (refreshed 5/28) was the template.
- **Graduation weekend is exactly the moment the Lafayette restaurants page gets visited from search** — out-of-town relatives looking up "where to eat in Lafayette CA" the day before they fly in. A page that until today was a flat bulleted list was a missed conversion. This fix lands before the bulk of the late-May/early-June graduation visitors finish booking.
